Notes & Assumptions

  • Erlang C: We solve for minimum agents N such that Service Level ≥ target for given arrival rate λ and AHT. Offered load A = λ·AHT/3600.
  • Shrinkage: Applied as gross-up on productive FTE. Forecast error adds a volume buffer on λ.
  • Abandonment: Estimated via exponential patience with median τ; P(abandon) ≈ 1 − exp(−ASA/τ).
  • FCR impact: Lower FCR increases repeat demand next period; we model a monthly multiplier on volume: 1/(FCR).
  • Economics: Cost per seat includes salary/overheads. Attrition cost = hires × training cost; hires = headcount × (attrition/12).
  • Agentic Recovery: Recovers a % of abandons and reduces churn on at-risk customers; value per save is user-defined.
